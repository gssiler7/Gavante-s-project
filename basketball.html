The three athletes below are all legends in the making. One has already been inducted into the NBA's Hall of Fame (Allen Iverson) and the other two are surely on their way to do the same. Being able to dominate the game is something that should not be taken lightly. The videos that you are about to watch are just a sample of what these guys are capable of. You will also notice a few of their achievements and awards that they have received for possessing such incredible skills.

Chef Curry

  • NBA Champion (2015)
  • NBA Most Valuable Player Award (2015)
  • NBA All-Star (2014-2016)
  • All-NBA Team (2015)
  • NBA Three-Point Contest Champion (2015)
  • Best Male Athlete ESPY Award (2015)
  • Best NBA Player ESPY Award (2015)

King James

  • NBA Champion (2012, 2013)
  • NBA Finals MVP (2012, 2013)
  • NBA Most Valuable Player Award (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
  • NBA All-Star (2005-2016)
  • NBA All-Star Game MVP (2006, 2008)
  • NBA Rookie of the Year (2004)
  • NBA Scoring Champion (2008)

The Answer

  • NBA Most Valuable Player Award (2001)
  • NBA All-Star (2000-2010)
  • NBA All-Star Game MVP (2001, 2005)
  • All NBA Team (1999, 2001, 2005)
  • NBA Rookie of the Year (1997)
  • NBA Rookie Challenge MVP (1997)
  • NBA Scoring Champion (1999, 2001, 2002, 2005)
  • NBA Steals Leader (2001-2003)
  • No. 3 retired by Philadelphia 76ers
  • Inducted into NBA Hall of Fame (2016)
